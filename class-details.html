Class Details

Class - 1: Start Overthink

In the "Start Overthink" class, students will learn the art of channeling their thoughts towards solving complex puzzles, analyzing various scenarios, and pushing their brain to achieve higher levels of cognitive efficiency.

The curriculum includes mental exercises, practical logic-building tasks, and engaging activities that enhance problem-solving skills. By the end of this class, students will not only overthink more effectively but also gain a better understanding of how their mind works.

Class - 2: Stop Overthink

The "Stop Overthink" class is tailored for those who find themselves trapped in a loop of unproductive thought patterns. This program dives into identifying the root causes of overthinking and provides actionable strategies to regain control of your mind.

Through meditation techniques, mindfulness exercises, and guided counseling, students will develop the tools to overcome their overthinking tendencies and focus on the present moment. Empower yourself to live a balanced, worry-free life.
